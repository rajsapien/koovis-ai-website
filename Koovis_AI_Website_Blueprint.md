## 8. DESIGN DIRECTION

### Aesthetic
- **Dark theme** (charcoal/near-black) — signals technical depth, premium
- **Accent: Warm amber or electric cyan** — pick one, commit fully
- **Typography**: A serif for headlines (authority, editorial weight) + geometric sans for body (modern, clean) + monospace for technical accents
- **Minimal decoration** — let whitespace, typography, and your numbers do the work
- **No stock photos** — geometric patterns, subtle grid/dot backgrounds, data visualization motifs
- **Mobile-first** — many founders will find you on LinkedIn → phone

### References to study
- https://linear.app — clean, dark, technical but accessible
- https://vercel.com — strong typography, confident copy
- https://resend.com — developer-focused, elegant
- https://cal.com — open, honest, clear value prop

## 9. TECH STACK RECOMMENDATION

### Frontend
- **Next.js 14+ (App Router)** with TypeScript
- **Tailwind CSS** for styling
- **Framer Motion** for animations
- **MDX** for blog posts (write in Markdown, render as React)

### Backend
- **Next.js API Routes** (serverless on Vercel)
- **Resend** for contact form emails (free tier: 100 emails/day)
- **Upstash Redis** for rate limiting contact form
- **Buttondown or ConvertKit** for newsletter

### Hosting & Deployment
- **Vercel** (free tier is plenty for launch)
- **GitHub repo** → auto-deploy on push
- **Namecheap DNS**: Point nameservers to Vercel's (ns1.vercel-dns.com, ns2.vercel-dns.com)

### Analytics
- **Vercel Analytics** (built-in, privacy-friendly)
- **Plausible** (optional, lightweight alternative to Google Analytics)
